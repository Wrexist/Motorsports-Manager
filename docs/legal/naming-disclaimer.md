# Naming and disclaimer checklist

Store-facing and marketing copy must avoid implying affiliation with any real racing series, teams, drivers, or circuits.

## Required disclaimer (short)

> Not affiliated with any real racing series, governing body, circuit, team, or driver.

## Checklist before store submission

- [ ] No use of protected series names or logos in screenshots or metadata.
- [ ] No real driver names or likenesses in-game.
- [ ] No real circuit names; use fictional circuit names only.
- [ ] Avoid real team three-letter codes and identity stacking (name + colors + implied nationality + stats).
- [ ] Credits / settings screen includes the disclaimer above.
